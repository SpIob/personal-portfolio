Hi! My name is Jan Mico Arenga. You may call me Mico or Jim. I work as an amateur YouTuber, started from 2022, and I am my own boss. I write the scripts and edit and record my own videos.

I use Lightworks as my main editing software, along with Avidemux, and Audacity for the audio editing. I mainly use Google Docs when writing the scripts. I use an iPhone 13 for recording videos, Vivo Y13 for the audio, and OBS for gameplay.

I use C, HTML, and CSS for the most part, therefore, I do web development stuff mostly.

I sometimes use Python and Java, because I'm still learning how to use them.

I am currently working on a fantasy world named Yharnanqii. It is a more grounded take on the fantasy genre, with a little hint of realism in there. The whole idea and progression is solely focused on the world itself, rather than the characters.
